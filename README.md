## Business Context
Olist is a Brazilian e-commerce marketplace that connects customers with thousands of independent sellers across the country.

Customer satisfaction is a key performance indicator for any marketplace, influencing customer retention, seller performance and long-term business growth.

This case study investigates the operational and commercial factors that influence customer satisfaction using Olist's public e-commerce dataset. The objective is to provide evidence-based recommendations that could support business decision-making.

At this stage, no assumptions have been made regarding the causes of customer satisfaction or dissatisfaction. The investigation will allow the data to guide the findings.

## Project Methodology
The project follows a structured analytical approach beginning with business understanding before progressing to data validation, exploration, analysis and business recommendations.

Each stage builds upon evidence gathered during the previous stage.
