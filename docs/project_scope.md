# Project Scope

## Background
Olist operates one of Brazil's largest e-commerce marketplaces, connecting customers with independent sellers across multiple product categories.

Understanding customer satisfaction is critical to maintaining marketplace performance and supporting long-term business growth.

---

## Objective
Investigate the operational and commercial factors associated with customer satisfaction using historical marketplace data.

---

## Scope
The analysis will examine:

- Customer reviews
- Delivery performance
- Product categories
- Seller performance
- Geographic trends
- Commercial indicators

---

## Expected Deliverables
- SQL exploration
- Python analysis
- Tableau dashboard
- Executive summary
- Business recommendations
