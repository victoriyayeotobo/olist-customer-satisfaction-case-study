# KPI Framework

## Purpose

The following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) have been identified based on the business objective of understanding the factors that influence customer satisfaction across the Olist marketplace.

These KPIs will be validated and calculated during the analysis phase of the project.

---

| KPI | Business Purpose | Primary Stakeholder |
|------|------------------|---------------------|
| Average Order Fulfilment Time | Measures the end-to-end customer delivery experience from purchase to delivery | Customer Experience Team |
| Average Order Approval Time | Measures the efficiency of order processing | Operations Team |
| Average Dispatch Time | Measures how quickly orders are handed over to carriers | Operations Team |
| Average Delivery Time | Measures logistics performance after carrier collection | Operations Team |
| Late Delivery Rate | Measures the percentage of orders delivered after the estimated delivery date | Executive Leadership, Operations |
| Average Customer Review Score | Measures overall customer satisfaction | Executive Leadership |
| Seller Performance Score | Measures seller performance using review scores and delivery metrics | Seller Success Team |
| Product Category Satisfaction | Identifies product categories associated with lower customer satisfaction | Product Team |
